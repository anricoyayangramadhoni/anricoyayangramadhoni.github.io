Pemdes Banjarmangu Terima Mahasiswa KKN UNSIQ

Banjarmangu - pemdes desa banjarmangu menyambut Mahasiswa UNSIQ yang akan melakukan kelompok kuliah pengabdian masyarakat (KPM) yang sering dikenal dengan istilah kuliah kerja nyata (KKN) dalam acara penerjunan mahasiswa KPM 44-03 progam studi ilmu politik 25 Desenber 2023 bertempet di balai desa banjarmangu.

Mahasiswa sebanyak 10 orang dari prodi S1 Ilmu Politik, Fakultas Komunikasi dan Sosial Politik akan melakukan KPM selama 40 hari di Desa Banjarmangu dimulai pada tanggal 23 Desenber 2023 sampai dengan 12 Februari 2024.

Mahasiswa UNSIQ Prodi S1 Ilmu Politik akan melaksanakan KPM di Desa Banjarmangu. Untuk teknis dan program KPM nya diserahkan kepada mahasiswa dan juga Pemdes Banjarmangu agar KPM bisa berjalan dengan lancar. KPM ini merupakan salah satu syarat kelulusan mahasiswa UNSIQ yang telah menyelesaikan seluruh mata kuliah‚Äù tuturnya.




Banjarmangu Village Government Accepts UNSIQ KKN Students

Banjarmangu - Banjarmangu village government welcomed UNSIQ students who will conduct community service lecture groups (KPM) which are often known as real work lectures (KKN) at the KPM 44-03 student launch event for the political science study program 25 December 2023 at the Banjarmangu village hall.

10 students from the Bachelor of Political Science study program, Faculty of Communication and Social Politics will carry out KPM for 40 days in Banjarmangu Village starting from 23 December 2023 to 12 February 2024.

UNSIQ Political Science Undergraduate Study Program students will carry out KPM in Banjarmangu Village. The technicalities and KPM program are handed over to students and also the Banjarmangu Village Government so that KPM can run smoothly. "KPM is one of the graduation requirements for UNSIQ students who have completed all courses," he said.
